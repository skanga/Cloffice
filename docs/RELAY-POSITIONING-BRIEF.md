# Relay Positioning Brief

Date: 2026-03-23
Status: Draft v1

## 1. Category
Relay is a governed AI cowork interface for business operations, powered by OpenClaw as the execution runtime.

## 2. One-Line Positioning
Relay helps teams move from AI conversation to controlled execution across real workflows, with human approval and auditability built in.

## 3. Who It Is For (ICP)
Primary ICP:

- Founder-led digital businesses with lean teams.
- Operations and finance leaders managing recurring decisions.
- Teams with fragmented data across chat, docs, CRM, tickets, and ERP.

Secondary ICP:

- Mid-market teams needing AI throughput without losing governance.
- Regulated or policy-sensitive functions requiring traceability.

## 4. Core Problem Statement
Most companies using AI face three gaps:

1. Chat does not equal execution.
2. Individual usage does not equal team workflow.
3. Productivity gains do not equal governed, auditable operations.

Relay solves these by combining a cowork UX with a backend orchestration runtime.

## 5. Value Proposition
Relay turns fragmented operational work into a plan-approve-execute loop:

- Plan with context.
- Review with rationale.
- Approve with policy controls.
- Execute across systems.
- Verify with receipts and audit history.

## 6. Differentiation Pillars
1. Architecture separation:
- Relay handles human interaction.
- OpenClaw handles orchestration and tool execution.

2. Human-in-the-loop by default:
- Consequential actions require explicit approval.

3. Team-native operations:
- Multi-user workflows, handoffs, and role-based escalations.

4. Policy-aware execution:
- Recommendations are tied to rules and thresholds.

5. Full traceability:
- Every run has a readable timeline and audit trail.

## 7. Positioning Against Status Quo
Status quo:

- Generic assistants, disconnected pilots, no stable operating model.

Relay:

- A repeatable operating interface for governed AI execution.

## 8. Messaging Framework
Hero message:

- AI coworker for operations, not just chat.

Support points:

- From suggestion to action in one thread.
- Human approval for high-impact decisions.
- Shared team context and auditable outcomes.
- Built on OpenClaw for controlled orchestration.

Proof themes:

- Reduced decision latency.
- Fewer context switches.
- Higher consistency in approvals and actions.
- Clear execution trace for stakeholders.

## 9. Elevator Pitch
Relay is the operator layer on top of OpenClaw. It gives teams a Claude-style cowork experience while keeping enterprise controls intact. Users can delegate real work across systems, approve key actions, and keep a complete record of what happened and why.

## 10. Positioning Guardrails
Do say:

- Governed AI operations interface.
- Human-led, AI-assisted execution.
- OpenClaw-powered orchestration runtime.

Do not say:

- Fully autonomous black-box AI.
- Another generic chatbot.
- Model lab or frontier model company.
